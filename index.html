Design Intelligence Creative Learning Synthetic Cognition

My work explores the integration of data-driven methods in creative learning and design practices with a focus on synthetic intelligence. I investigate how data-driven frameworks can evaluate creativity in architecture and adjacent fields, while examining how Artificial Intelligence is reshaping both the learning process and the built environments it influences.
